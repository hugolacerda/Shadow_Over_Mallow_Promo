Synopsis

A lone martial artist (Hugo Lacerda) is lost on his commute back home and is forced to take refuge in an eerie tavern inn for the night. After a mysterious flat tire on his bike strands him to the inn, followed shortly by the sudden disappearance of his phone, the lone batler begins to suspect something mischievous and evil is afoot. It doesn't take long for him to find out that the tavern isn't as it seems as he finds himself in havan of human sacrificing cultists. (The EvilBob0).
