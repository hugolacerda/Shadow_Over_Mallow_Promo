A lone mortail artist (Hugo Lacerda) is lost on his comute back home and is forced to take refuge in an errie tavern innn for the night. After a mysterious flat tire on his bike strands him to the inn, followed shortly by the sudden disapearance of his phone, the lone battler begins to suspect something mischevious and evil is afoot. It doesn't take long for him to find out that the tavern isn't as it seems as he finds himself in havan of human sacrificing cultists (The EvilBob0).

Now the lone warrior must put aside his fear and fight for the salvation of his own life and the friends and allies he's made along the way in an epic horror with an unpredicatable twist ending. Featuring the combined skill of The EvilBob0 production team and music by TJ Welches, and staring Hugo Lacerda as the lone martial artists, tonight there is a Shadow Over Mallow.
